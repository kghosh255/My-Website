Recently Funded Projects of Kuntal Ghosh as PI in the domain of Machine Learning and Knowledge Graphs


1. Title: Analysis of influential features for mapping landslides in different vulnerable zones in India (2024-27)

Funding: ISI

Objective: Analyzing specific landslide and land erosion prone regions like some localized areas in north-west and north-east, India using higher resolution images from ISRO’s Resourcesat LISS IV MX is planned to be used to derive the features at a higher spatial resolution of 5m, with the help of machine learning.

Publications: Neogi, S., Aich, G., Dey, A. et al. (2024) Otsu-BRSG: An Effective Algorithm for River Bank Line Detection and Monitoring in the Challenging Terrains of Kaziranga National Park. J Indian Soc Remote Sens 52: 1–20. https://doi.org/10.1007/s12524-024-01843-z
